Recipe #1

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 1-2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 1
Salt 1 pinch
Sugar 50g
Milk 150mg
Vegetable oil 60mg
Flour 150mg
Baking powder 1 tsp
Butter 20g
Banana 1

Step 1: Mix the egg, salt and sugar in one container.

Step 2: Add milk, vegetable oil. We mix.

Step 3: Add flour and baking powder, mix again.

Step 4: Lubricate the pan with butter by hand.

Step 5: Pour the resulting mixture into the pan.

Step 6: On top of the mixture, lay down the cut pieces of banana.

Step 7: Cover the lid of the pan and put on the slowest fire for 20 minutes (leave until the dough sticks to your hands)

Step 8: Turn the dough upside down and set for 5-7 minutes.

The dish is ready to eat.

Source: Banana Pie

Recipe #2

  • Time: 40min
  • Serves: 1 people
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 2
Soda 1 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Milk 100ml
Flour 1,5 cups
Butter 80g
Banana 3

Step 1: Mash bananas into a porridge and mix with butter, eggs and sugar.

Step 2: Then add flour and soda, pour in milk.

Step 3: Bake for 30 minutes at 200 degrees.

Source: Banana Pie with Milk

Recipe #3

  • Time: 1h
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 3
Sugar 1,5 cups
Vegetable oil 1/3
Flour 4-5 tablespoon
Baking powder 2 tsp
Banana 4

Step 1: Peel bananas and mash with a fork.

Step 2: Add sugar and mix.

Step 3: Add eggs and mix.

Step 4: Add flour with baking powder and mix.

Step 5: Add oil. Mix thoroughly.

Step 6: Leave the dough to ripen for 20-30 minutes. At this time, preheat the oven to 180C. Lubricate the mold with oil.

Step 7: Bake for 45-50 minutes until done.

Source: Banana Pie
